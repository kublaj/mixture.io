Free

As of today the Mixture application will become a free download.

Unfortunately this does come at a price, we are also removing the subscription / pro plan over the next 2 months.

If you are a pro user of Mixture you should have received a mail – but please read on…

How will you be affected?

We will no longer be actively developing the application but we will continue to maintain its current feature set and push updates when possible.

If you have a pro account, we have cancelled your subscription and payment with immediate effect and are giving you 2 months to migrate any projects you may have hosted with Mixture.

Once the 2 month period has passed, we will roll out the new free version of the application as an auto-update for all current users (this update will remove any dependencies to the online services).

We understand that for some pro users this is not ideal, but we hope you understand that it is necessary.

We use Mixture and the online service continuously ourselves and so this has not been an easy decision, we’ve looked long and hard at the options, but it’s simply not viable to continue in its current form.

Please note

If you would like to continue using the publish options for the duration of the 2 month notice period, please skip any requests to update the app as the new version has been heavily updated and the online features removed.

We want to thank all of our users and everyone that has supported Mixture.

Neil & Pete
